Can you think of an analogy to describe HTML, CSS and the DOM? How would you describe it to your non-technical friend?

HTML is the written text/content on a page, while CSS is the design of the page where images and layouts/colours can be coded in. DOM is the storyboard of the whole website.

What is the box model ?

Box model is the design and layout of a webpage, where there is images or text there will be a box around the content.

What is meant by boxifying design

Boxifying design comes from websites general structure of everything being a box. Where there is a group of text and pictures there will be a box around that content.
